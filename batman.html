Batman Rogues Gallery

The Rogues Gallery is the term for the various villains that Batman has faced over the years.
These have ranged from costumed villains like the Joker and Two-Face, crime bosses like Rupert Thorne,Sal Maroni, and Carmine Falcone, to lesser known foes such as Magpie and Humpty Dumpty.
